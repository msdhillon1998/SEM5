Welcome to Paradise

Comments about a recent stay at a Paradise hotel? Send us an email or call 1-800-PARADISE (1-800-445-8667) and ask for Guest Assistance. Prefer help on social media? Ask a question through Facebook or Twitter. We're here 24/7 and strive to provide a reply within an hour.

Call Us

For Online Reservation assistance, contact our Internet Reservations desk at 1-800-774-1500, from within the U.S. and Canada, or TDDY/TTY 1-800-368-1133. Customers outside the US may dial 00-1-800-774-1500, your call will be charged at international direct dial rates. To make Paradise Reservations by phone and you are calling from the U.S., Canada, Puerto Rico or the U.S. Virgin Islands, call 1-800-445-8667. Otherwise, contact the local reservations office for your region.
